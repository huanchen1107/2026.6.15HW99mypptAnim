● Yes — that skill supports PDF directly. Its description covers "image-only
  slide deck (NotebookLM-style PPTX/PDF where every slide is one flat image)",
  so a PDF where each page is a flat slide image works the same way as a PPTX:
  it converts each page to an image, segments the elements (cards, arrows,
  charts, highlight groups), syncs TTS narration, and renders the MP4.

  The one caveat: it's designed for image-style slides (like NotebookLM
  exports). If your PDF is a regular text document (a report, paper, etc.), it
  will still treat each page as one flat picture — it won't reflow text into
  proper slides, so the result may look like narrated page scans rather than a
  designed video.

  If you have a PDF ready, just point me at it (e.g. "make a video from
  slides.pdf") and I'll invoke the skill.
