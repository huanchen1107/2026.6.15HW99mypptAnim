## Slide 01 - Designing the Future of Work

We are moving from narrow AI to general AI, and that changes how we think about work itself. In this deck, you will see two complementary paths: vibe coding and workflow automation. Together, they point toward a future where humans guide systems, not just operate tools.

## Slide 02 - The Evolution of AI Execution

The old model was a single task: you asked, you executed, and you repeated. The new model is a loop of understanding, decomposing, using tools, checking results, and finishing the job. That shift is what makes AI useful for real work, not just isolated prompts.

## Slide 03 - Direction 1: Vibe Coding

Vibe coding replaces manual coding and interface tweaking with natural language instructions. Instead of editing every detail yourself, you describe the intent and let AI translate that intent into execution. This makes the operator more like a creative director than a hands-on mechanic.

## Slide 04 - Vibe Coding Transforms the Operator into a Director

On the left, the traditional operator is trapped inside a messy, fixed environment. On the right, the director shapes the outcome by guiding design, programming, content, and analysis. The key change is not just speed, but a new kind of control over the work.

## Slide 05 - Direction 2: Workflow Automation

Workflow automation is about completing the whole job, step by step, with almost no human intervention until final quality control. Instead of copying text or manually pasting between tools, the system handles a structured flow from input to output. That is how complex, multi-stage work becomes repeatable.

## Slide 06 - Automating the Process: Educational Video Production

This slide shows a full production pipeline, from PDF to summary lines, script, storyboard, microphone, subtitles, and final review. The point is that AI can now coordinate an end-to-end process, not just generate a single answer. In practical terms, it can help create polished educational videos with far less manual glue work.

## Slide 07 - Two Paths to the Future of Work

The two paths answer different questions. Vibe coding asks, “How do I use this tool?” while workflow automation asks, “How does the entire job get done?” One changes the human role from operator to director, and the other changes the human role from user to flow designer.

## Slide 08 - The Synthesis: The Ultimate AI System

The real future is not choosing between these two ideas, but combining them. Vibe coding handles the individual steps, while workflow automation structures the sequence around them. Put together, they form a true AI system that can execute, coordinate, and improve.

## Slide 09 - The 8-Step System Design Blueprint

Building that system starts with a clear blueprint: find a real scenario, analyze the current flow, identify AI opportunities, design the interaction, design the workflow, build a prototype, test and iterate, then present and reflect. Each step makes the system more real and more usable. That is how an AI user becomes an AI workflow designer.

## Slide 10 - The Next-Generation Core Competencies

The next generation of work needs new core skills: professional expression, prompt design, task deconstruction, workflow design, and quality control. These are the abilities that let people steer AI with clarity and confidence. The goal is no longer just using AI well, but designing systems that work well.
